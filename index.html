STYLE GUIDE

INTRODUCTION

JCN styles guidelines are based on earthy colors and clean shapes. The palette I chose, has warm colors.
The idea of nature brings me feelings of openness. I like to be open to new possibilities and keep my feet on the ground. The color palette I selected represent me best in so many areas of my personality and way of thinking.
My fonts selection started with the search for clean shapes. I was debating between Lato and Ubunto and ended up deciding Ubuntu. After choosing Ubuntu, I wanted a Serif font that could pair with it and Merriweather was the perfect match.
The layout ideas, I like empty spaces in between elements. I try to keep some air and cleanliness where I can make it work. In my mood board, I added some examples of content layout that goes best with what I want to show in my portfolio.
